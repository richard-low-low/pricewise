PriceWise Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

In short: PriceWise does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

What data does PriceWise store?

  • Price history — product prices you encounter while browsing Amazon, stored locally in your browser
  • Price alerts — target prices you set for products, stored locally
  • Product metadata — title, image URL, and ASIN for products you visit, stored locally

Where is the data stored?

All data is stored exclusively in your browser using chrome.storage.local. No data is sent to any external server, API, or third party.

What data does PriceWise NOT collect?

  • No personal information (name, email, account details)
  • No browsing history beyond Amazon product pages
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No data shared with advertisers or third parties

Affiliate links

PriceWise does not modify, inject, or hijack any affiliate links on Amazon. We do not participate in any affiliate program.

Permissions explained

  • storage — to save price history and alerts locally
  • alarms — to periodically check prices for your alerts
  • notifications — to notify you when a price drops to your target
  • tabs — to check prices in background tabs for your active alerts
  • host_permissions (amazon.*) — to read product prices on Amazon pages

Data deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Removing individual products from the popup
  • Uninstalling the extension (all data is automatically deleted)

Open source

PriceWise is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify these claims.
