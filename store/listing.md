# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name

PriceWise - Amazon Price Tracker

## Short Description (132 chars)

Track Amazon prices privately. See price history charts, set drop alerts. No data collection, no affiliate hijacking.

## Detailed Description

PriceWise tracks Amazon product prices and shows you a clear price history chart right on the product page. It works across 9 Amazon domains (US, UK, DE, FR, JP, CA, AU, IT, ES) and supports multiple currencies.

**What you get:**

- Price history chart displayed directly on Amazon product pages
- Lowest, highest, and average price statistics at a glance
- Price drop alerts -- set a target price and get notified when it drops
- Support for 9 Amazon regional stores and their currencies
- Up to 2 years of personal price tracking history

**Privacy first -- no exceptions:**

- All price data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
- Zero data collection. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.
- No affiliate link hijacking. PriceWise never modifies or injects affiliate codes.
- Minimal permissions. Only accesses Amazon product pages, nothing else.

**Why PriceWise instead of other trackers?**

Popular price trackers come with serious drawbacks. Honey was caught hijacking affiliate links from content creators, replacing their referral codes with its own. Keepa requires account creation and raises privacy concerns by collecting browsing data.

PriceWise takes a different approach: your data stays on your device, the code is open source, and the extension does exactly one thing well -- tracking prices transparently.

**Free and open source.**

PriceWise is completely free to use. The source code is publicly available for anyone to inspect, audit, or contribute to.
